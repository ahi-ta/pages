AHI-TA! Privacy Policy

User Data Policy

We use a sign up form to register your email and name in order to provide better recommendations, service and experience.

We do not use your information for anything that does not involve providing a better experience while using our tool.

We do not sell or share any of your personal data or use it for other projects or applications.

What kind of information do we handle and/or collect?

  1. We retrieve the product you select on our supported retailers in order to provide you with recommendations for better purchasing experience.
  2. We save your cart selections in order to improve our recommendations in future purchases.

Retailers Data Collection Agreement

By using our plugin you agree to share the prices of products on our supported retailers' sites to AHI-TA! so that we can provide recommendations to other users using the plugin.
